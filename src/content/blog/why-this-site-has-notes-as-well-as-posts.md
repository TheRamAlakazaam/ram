---
title: Why This Site Has Notes As Well As Posts
description: Making room for smaller publishing formats keeps the whole site more alive.
publishDate: 2026-03-24
tags:
  - content strategy
  - notes
  - blogging
---

If every update has to become a polished article, a site goes quiet.

Notes solve that. They create a space for drafts, fragments, short lists,
micro-observations, and links that matter today even if they do not deserve two
thousand words.

That distinction helps with maintenance too. I can publish more often without
confusing readers who came for longer essays or more technical writing.
