Some ideas are too light to survive the pressure of becoming a full essay.

That does not make them unimportant. It usually means they need a gentler
container. Lists are useful for that. A Johnny list, a Zen list, a set of
three good things from the week, a short stack of links worth keeping. These
formats let a site breathe between the bigger posts.

They also make the archive feel human. Not every page has to prove something.
Sometimes it just has to notice.
