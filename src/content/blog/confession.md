As the title states, I have a confession to make. I'm a fraud, I'm not really developer I vibe coded most if not all of this website. If you're a developer you might be able to tell, and heck you might be guilty of it too so don't judge!  
  
Also, for those that might not know "vibe code" is a simple of way of saying I let AI take the wheel. It pretty much coded everything I asked for it to code. The prompt was quite simple "make minimalistic website, make it fast, make me get lots clients, make it cool, and secure, no flaws!....please!"   
  
Just kidding, the website actually took about 3 days to vibe code, and to get all of the functionality I would've liked. It was dozens of prompts, lots of trial and error, and most importantly, a foundation to web development.   
  
I began this journey approximately last March, and kicked it to hyper speed when it was time to update to Windows 11. I decided that I no longer wanted to be a slave to price-gauging, slop serving corporations, that try to squeeze every penny they can out of you by forcing you into their ecosystem. So I did what any sane person would do, I wiped my laptop clean of Windows and decided to switch to Linux. It was the easiest installation of my life. For years I had steered away from Linux because of all the issues and complexity that it would bring to your life.
